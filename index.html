The unbonding pressure gauge is a composite 0–100 score built from on-chain data:

Supply (0–40): Compares the upcoming 7-day unlock volume against the full schedule average. Tomorrow's unlock is weighted more heavily than day 7.

Concentration (0–20): Measures whether unlocks are clustered on a single day or spread evenly. Spikes increase the score.

Behavior (0–40): Tracks what happens after ATOM unbonds — held, re-staked, sent to exchanges, or bridged. This signal calibrates over 7 days of observed data.

This is on-chain data analysis only — not financial advice. Always do your own research.
